OUR STORY

Rooted in Kyoto,
Grown in Tokyo

Mori means forest in Japanese. We wanted to create a small forest in the middle of the city — a place where you can breathe, slow down, and savor the moment. My grandma started this idea back in 2022. I'm just finishing it.

We directly import our matcha from a 4th-generation farm in Uji, stone-milled, shade-grown 21 days. No powders, no shortcuts.
